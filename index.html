About

I’m a Software Engineering (BSc) graduate and current MSc Software Engineering student with a passion for software development and graphic design. I work as a System Administrator and a Surveillance Operator, where I manage and optimize IT systems, while also pursuing web and mobile app development projects and creating visual designs. By combining my professional experience with creative and technical skills, I deliver practical, user-focused solutions that look as good as they work.

My Offerings

  • Web Development

    Frontend, backend, API, and full-stack development services to create dynamic and responsive websites and applications.

  • Graphic Design

    Logo design, artwork, and visual branding services to create compelling graphics that effectively communicate your message and enhance your brand identity.

  • Portrait Photography

    Editing and retouching services to enhance the quality and visual appeal of your photos, ensuring they look their best for personal or professional use.

  • Editorial Typography
